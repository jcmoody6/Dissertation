SEM model files (Mplus) relating car pride and household car ownership. Included in the following publication:

Moody, J., and J. Zhao. (2019). Car pride and its bidirectional relation with car ownership: Case studies in New York City and Houston. Transportation Research Part A: Policy and Practice, 124: 334-353. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.tra.2019.04.005

For measurement models of the car pride and general pride latent variables included in the analysis, see the folder "Chpt3_ExplicitMeasure".
